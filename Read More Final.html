Introduction

PubliToilet is a website that follows Sustainable Development Goal (SDG) 6, which aims to ensure availability and sustainable management of sanitation for all. The website provides resources and information to promote access to clean and safe public toilets, thereby contributing to better hygiene and health outcomes in communities.

Sustainable Development Goal 6

SDG 6 is necessary for the public's human health. Human health is directly influenced by access to clean water and sanitation, which are essential components of Sustainable Development Goal 6. Ensuring availability and sustainable management of water resources prevents waterborne diseases and promotes overall well-being in communities.

How Our Website Helps Promote SDG 6?

Our website helps promote Sustainable Development Goal 6 (SDG 6) by improving access to better sanitation. This includes ensuring that public bathrooms are clean, allowing users to determine whether the bathroom is properly cleaned. This feature allows users to make informed decisions about where to go and also encourages facilities to maintain higher cleanliness standards. By fostering accountability and awareness, we contribute to achieving SDG 6 and ensuring access to safe and hygienic sanitation for all.
