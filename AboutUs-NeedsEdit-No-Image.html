PubliToilet is a student-led project from FEU Tech’s College of Computer Studies that helps users locate nearby public toilets, rate cleanliness, report issues, and learn hygiene practices. We support SDG 6 through maps, filters, reports, and education.

Our Mission

To provide a user-centered platform for clean, accessible toilets using real-time tools, reporting, and hygiene education in line with SDG 6.

Our Vision

To build an inclusive community with clean, accessible toilets for all—supporting a sustainable and equitable future.
